At Narrabundah college, you have the opportunity to invest in yourself and your future.

Narrabundah College offers a challenging curriculum in a caring environment and meets the needs of an international community.

Our College has earned an enviable record of student achievement in academic, sporting, social and cultural endeavours.

A high percentage of students go on to further education at universities and specialist tertiary institutions.

This school has a very strong international atmosphere: indeed our students come from many different cultural backgrounds with fifty-nine countries of origin and fifty-five different first languages spoken.

Narrabundah College provides:

  • An environment where you as a student can discover your strengths, develop your potential and excel
  • An harmonious environment in which you can thrive academically
  • Targeted and strong student support over your 2 years at the college
  • Opportunities for students to develop into global citizens
  • Strong partnerships with parents/carers and the community.
