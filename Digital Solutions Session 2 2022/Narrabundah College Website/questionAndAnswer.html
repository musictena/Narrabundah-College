Q and A

╰──➢ How are you marked?:

For Session 1; there will be an assignment and an end of session test.

For Season 2, there will be two assignments and an end of session test.

For Session 3, there will be one assignment and one end of session test.

*this may vary between each Infomation Technology subject and teachers

╰──➢ Student Answer: “Are IT subjects difficult?”

"All subjects begin at a beginner-level difficulty, but the pace at which each subject proceeds differs.

For subjects such as Digital Solutions, the pace moves at a normal-like, where the speed varies depending on the student. In terms of studying, it is mainly self-study.
For Robotics and Mechatronics, the speed is slightly faster than Digital Solutions but it is relatively easy to grasp.
For Networking and Cybersecurity,the speed is pretty much the same speed as Robotics and Mechatronics."

╰──➢ What is the difficulty of each subject?

Our three subjects are all made beginner-friendly, but after the first session, topics will increase
in difficulty, based on the previous session's work. This may mean that students enrolling in the
subject for the first time (in session 2) may struggle to learn the new content.
